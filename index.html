Iconic trucks in the media

Farmers all over the world love them. No muddy terrain can stop or slow down them. no matter how, or where or when, they do what they have been designed for: offer you the best experience you will ever have. And the hundreds of horses under the hood are neighing and calling you for the wildest drive of your life, day after day. Every time they appear in a movie, they become the main hero. Let's see some old ads of some classy trucks from the past that continue to live in the present and most likely in the foreseeable future.
